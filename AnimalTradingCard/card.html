Tiger

 

Land development has led to around 96 per cent of the tiger’s natural range being lost in past 100 years.

  • Weigh: Up to 300 kilograms (660 pounds)– that’s about the same as ten ten year olds!
  • Length: Up to 3.3 metres (11 feet).
  • Subspecies: Sumatran Tiger, Siberian Tiger, Bengal Tiger, South China Tiger, Malayan Tiger and Indochinese Tiger.
  • Breed: Tigers that breed with lions give birth to hybrids known as tigons and ligers.

The tiger is the largest cat species, most recognizable for their pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside. The species is classified in the genus Panthera with the lion, leopard, jaguar, and snow leopard.
